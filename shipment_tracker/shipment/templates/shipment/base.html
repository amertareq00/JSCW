About us

Based in the vibrant city of Amman, Jordan Shipping Company is a trusted and reliable logistics provider specializing in internal shipping within the cities of Jordan. With a deep understanding of the local market and a strong network of connections, they offer efficient and seamless transportation solutions to businesses and individuals across the country. Whether it's delivering goods from Amman to Aqaba or from Irbid to Petra, Jordan Shipping Company takes pride in their expertise and attention to detail. Their dedicated team ensures that each shipment is handled with care, from pickup to final delivery, providing timely and secure services that cater to the unique needs of their clients. With their extensive knowledge of the local roads, regulations, and customs procedures, Jordan Shipping Company is the go-to partner for reliable and hassle-free internal shipping in Jordan.
